Ikiwa MOLA wangu ananiuliza "una aibu yoyote kwa kuniasi?".
Unaficha dhambi zako kwa uumbaji wangu, na kwa dhambi unakuja kwangu ".
Kwa hivyo nitajibuje? Ole wangu, ni nani atanilinda?
Ninaendelea kuizuia roho yangu kwa mawazo ya matumaini mara kwa mara.
Na mimi husahau kitakachokuja baada ya kifo, kitakachokuja baada ya kukafinwa.
Kama kwamba nimehakikishiwa maisha ya milele na kwamba mauti hayatakuja kwangu.
Na ukali wa kifo utakaponipata, nani atanilinda.
Nikiangalia nyuso zao, je! Hakuna kati yao atakayenikomboa?
Nitaulizwa kuhusu kile nimeandaa katika maisha yangu kuniokoa (siku ya hukumu).
Halafu nitajibuje baada ya kupuuzia dini yangu.
Ole wangu! Sijasikia hotuba ya Mwenyezi Mungu ikinialika?
Sikusikia kile kilichoingia (sura) za QAF na YAASIN?
Je! Sikusikia habari ya siku ya kukusanyika, Siku ya Hukumu.
Sikusikia muitaji wa kifo akinialika, akiniita?
Kwa hivyo Mola wangu Mlezi, mtumwa (nimekugeukia), nimeitubu kwa hivyo nani atanihifadhi?
Namtarajia Mola katika msamaha- Kwa ukweli ataniongoza.
Nimekujia (kwa kujirudisha), kwa hivyo unirehemu, na ufanye mizani yangu iwe nzito (na vitendo vizuri).
Na nipunguzie akaunti yangu - Wewe ndiye bora zaidi ya wale ambao watanileta hesabu.



If my Lord asks me "have you (any) shame in disobeying Me?".
You conceal your sins from my creation, and with sins you come to me".
So how will I answer? O woe to me, and who shall protect me?
I keep averting my soul with thoughts of hope from time to time.
And I forget what is to come after death, what is to come after am shrouded.
As if am guaranteed life (eternally) and that death will not come to me.
And when the severity of death overtakes me, who will protect me.
I looked at the faces, is there not from amongst them who will ransom me?
I will be asked regarding what I have prepared in my life to save me (on the day of judgement).
Then how will I answer after I have neglected my religion.
Woe to me! Did I not hear the speech of Allah inviting me?
Did I not hear what came in (the chapters) of QAF and YAASIN?
Did I not hear about the of gathering, the day of Judgement.
Did I not hear the caller of death inviting me, calling me?
So O my Lord, a slave (turning to you), I have repented so who then shall shelter me?
Expect Lord extensive in forgiveness - To the truth He will Guide me.
I have come to You (in repetance), so have mercy on me,and make my scales heavy (with good deeds).
And lighten my account - You are the best of those who will bring me to account.
